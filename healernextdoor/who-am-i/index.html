Who Am I?

journeyNovember 4, 2021By Lisa Batitto

Hey hey! Are you new around here? Or maybe you’ve been following me for a while but still wonder… Who is this healer?

Well, here are a few fast facts:

1. I am an intuitive Reiki Master, a spiritual life coach, and a meditation teacher. This is a fancy way of saying I work with people who want to become their best selves.

2. Before pursuing the healing life, I worked in the public relations departments for nonprofits and arts organizations. I was always interested in spirituality though and decided it was time to answer my calling.

3. I’m not a fan of labels but I consider myself a Christian mystic. I also love learning about other spiritual practices and incorporating them into my work, especially Sufism.

Is there anything else you’d like to know? Just ask!
