Meet Your Spiritual Team

LearnApril 22, 2021By Lisa Batitto

Imagine that you have an entire team behind you that has your best interests at heart, always supports you, and loves you unconditionally.

Guess what? You do! I am partnering with my friend Trece Spalten to help you discover the love, guidance, and support that surrounds you always!

Opening to Your Spirit Guides reunites you with your personal squad of magical badasses, holy helpers, and wise companions who are totally invested in your well-being so you can live a more inspired, purposeful, and charmed life.

After a year of lockdowns, quarantines, and isolation, we are starting to emerge with a desire to connect with others, and yet also feel some awkwardness and fear around that. Many of us, especially the more sensitive and intuitive folks, are wondering:

~ Will I be able to connect with people in a way that feels good to me and isn't overwhelming?

~ How can I navigate my life in this world that seems to be constantly changing from one upheaval to the next?

~ What can I do to maintain my center and my peace of mind when there is so much divisiveness and tension out there?

The good news is that these questions and challenges are *exactly* what your Spirit Guides were made for!!

In Opening to Your Spirit Guides we will:

~ Empower the Authentic Self and strengthen our energetic boundaries so we can fully and enthusiastically show up in the world, and in our lives.

~ Learn about the different types of Guides and their sacred roles as part of your spirit squad (healers, teachers, guardians, ancestors, and divinities are a few of the Guides we will meet).

~ Have an embodied, experiential connection with the Guides that brings their presence more fully into our daily lives.

~ Find the healing, guidance, magic, and miracles we we need to flourish and thrive right when we seek it, no matter what is happening in the world.

Your Spirit Guides have been busy behind the scenes doing their best to bring you everything you need for a fulfilling and inspired life. Opening to Your Spirit Guides clears the way for them to become the helpers, healers, and friends you have been seeking.
