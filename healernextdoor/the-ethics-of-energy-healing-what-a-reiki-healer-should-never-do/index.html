The Ethics of Energy Healing: What a Reiki Healer Should Never Do

healingSeptember 19, 2023By Lisa Batitto

If you're searching for a Reiki healer to accompany you on your journey to well-being, you're in the right place. I'm here to help you understand the ethical side of energy healing so you can confidently choose the perfect healer for your needs. So, let's dive in and talk about the things you should never see from a Reiki healer.

Respect Those Boundaries: First and foremost, let's chat about boundaries. A good Reiki healer should only push you into things you’re comfortable with. Your journey is yours, and your comfort and consent are vital. Healing is a partnership, and a trustworthy healer will always value your input, ensuring you're a willing participant in your healing adventure.

Informed and Happy: Before your energy healing session, a responsible healer will take the time to explain the process, its potential benefits, and any possible side effects. They'll ask for your informed consent to make sure you know what to expect. You should never feel pressured into a session or left in the dark about how it all works.

No Magical Guarantees: Here's the real deal: Ethical Reiki healers will never promise you the moon and stars. Healing is a unique and intricate journey influenced by many factors. While energy healing can be truly transformative, it's crucial to be realistic about what it can and can't do.

It’s Personal: Trust is the heart of any healing connection. A Reiki healer should only spill the beans on your personal stories, experiences, or conversations with anyone else with your clear permission. Your privacy should be sacred and respected at all times.

Professionalism Always: A reputable Reiki healer should always act with professionalism and integrity. They should have the right training and qualifications, be upfront about their fees, and provide a cozy and secure space for your healing journey. You should feel right at home during your sessions.

Dollars and Cents Transparency: Ethical healers should be upfront about their fees and billing practices. There should be no surprises or sneaky extra charges. You deserve to know exactly what you're paying for and why.

The Quest for Knowledge: The world of energy healing is constantly evolving. A responsible Reiki healer will be dedicated to their growth and development. They'll stay updated with the latest practices, research, and ethical standards.

Respect Your Beliefs: Energy healing is a wonderfully diverse realm, and folks from all walks of life seek its benefits. A conscientious Reiki healer should honor your beliefs and never try to impose their own on you. They should create a space where you feel safe and supported, no matter your faith or spirituality.

No Strings Attached: An ethical Reiki healer will empower you to be self-sufficient on your healing journey. They should never encourage dependency or make you think you can only find healing through their services. Instead, they should inspire you to discover and nurture your inner strengths.

Self-Care First: A responsible healer understands the importance of self-care. They should never overstretch themselves or take on more clients than they can handle. Their well-being directly affects their ability to support you effectively.
