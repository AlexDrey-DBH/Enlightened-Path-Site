Preparing for a New Normal

journeyFebruary 22, 2021By Lisa Batitto

We are coming up on the anniversary of the year that changed the world and, in the process, our lives. We’ve all suffered losses and since we are still in the midst of this crisis, most of us haven’t even thought about healing yet. Have you thought about how you will adjust and thrive in our new normal?⠀
⠀
A teacher who showed me how to persevere and grow during hard times is Laura Day. Laura’s book Welcome to Your Crisis is my lighthouse when the waters get rough. It helped me see the opportunities and lessons in even the darkest moments. It’s one of the tools I used on my path to becoming a healer.⠀
⠀
