Mindset + Action = Manifesting Your Desires

journeyDecember 6, 2022By Lisa Batitto

So you think you understand the principles of manifesting, but you can’t understand why these things are still not coming to you. You believe that we create our reality. You know the laws of attraction and believe that what we think about we attract into our experience. You have even been practicing these principles, and that is when you remember to. When you think about it, you are repeating affirmations such as “my perfect relationship is available, and I deserve it.” “My income level is constantly increasing.” Okay, why is that ideal relationship or more income not what you're experiencing?

You may be trying to blame your parents for instilling some limiting belief that money is bad or that there is something spiritual about suffering. It doesn't matter where some of your beliefs come from or how many of them you have. There is a way around this. You need to focus on the beliefs that serve you better, such as “the Universe is full of abundance.” “The Universe wants to bring my desires to me.” You may be feeling unworthy. If you don't think you deserve to be happy and prosperous, how can you create that in your life? 

Okay, so now you are 1) believing that it IS possible to have all you desire. And 2) you are now feeling worthy. You feel like you have done your part, and now you are just waiting for the Universe to fulfill those desires. There is another little piece here that could be the missing link. Take action. But wait a minute, here you say. I thought I just had to ask and know that the Universe would supply, and presto! It appears. Not so fast; let’s see how taking action can assist in this process.

When you take action, any action, in the direction of your desire, you are showing the Universe that you are serious. It also facilitates the process because you focus your attention on your action instead of waiting. This action does not need to be huge, earth-shattering stuff. This action can come from a feeling of inspiration, intuition, or even coincidental events. One essential thing to remember when you take action is to feel good about it about yourself and what you want. Now you know how to assist the Universe in fulfilling your dreams.
