Ring in the New, Celebrate the Old

journeyDecember 20, 2022By Lisa Batitto

If you are anything like me, you’ve already started setting your intentions for the new year and putting action behind them. I wanted to share with you some of the things I have in store for the new year, all of which grew from seeds that were planted in 2022.

  • In addition to doing healings at Blu Lotus in Montclair, I will have office space at 33 Plymouth St., Montclair, on Wednesdays, beginning in January. I’m excited to share space with wellness and health practitioners and also to offer morning and evening hours to my clients. The space is accessible and has free parking. You can start booking appointments now.

  • I was thrilled with the reaction to my Enlightened Path Healing candle line, and the first edition has sold out. I am happy to announce that I am once again partnering with Nostalgia Candle Co. to create another batch of Reiki and full moon-infused candles. Stay tuned for details about how you can order.

  • This year, my first class of Reiki 1 and 2 students graduated. If you are interested in studying with me, I’m going to be offering Reiki Levels 1 & 2 and Master-Teacher level classes in the new year. If you haven’t already, sign up for my newsletter so you can be notified when classes are scheduled.

  • I’m wrapping up my year-long Consciousness Advisor training, and I can’t wait to offer this next level of service. The training has been led by Dr. Tony Nadar, the leader of the Transcendental Meditation movement, and I will be among the first graduates of this program. Certification day is January 12 and afterward. I’ll let you know how you can access tools to guide you along your path to enlightenment.
