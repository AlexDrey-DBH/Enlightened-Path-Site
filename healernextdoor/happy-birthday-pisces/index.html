Happy Birthday Pisces!

astrologyFebruary 19, 2021By Lisa Batitto

Do you have a friend who knows something is wrong before you even say hello?

Chances are pretty good that your pal is a Pisces - or has the fish floating around other areas of their astrological chart. If you ever are playing “guess my sign,” the easiest way to root out a Pisces is to say: "You look into someone's eyes and see their soul." While the other signs are looking at you with befuddlement, the fishies will be nodding solemnly in agreement.

Being this emotionally connected to others and feeling everything deeply, means Pisces are incredibly open with everyone and themselves. A Pisces just doesn’t wear their hearts on their sleeves, it’s stapled to the front of their shirt.

It’s no surprise then that people born under this sign are very romantic. Example: Elizabeth Barrett Browning. She wrote one of her most famous poems, How Do I Love Thee, for her husband.

Because Pisces are so generous with their love, for the next month we all get to share their energy. You can use it to manifest more romance in your relationship, to write a beautiful song or poem, or to connect more deeply with family and friends.

Happy birthday to all the fish out there
