healingJanuary 19, 2025By Lisa Batitto

I’m thrilled to share that I’m now offering Akashic Records readings. This has been a deeply personal journey for me, and after feeling called to this work and dedicating myself to study and practice, I’m honored to bring this experience to you.

During a session, I connect with your records to offer clarity, guidance, and insights on a situation or area where you seek understanding.

I’m offering 30-minute sessions for $80 or a 30-minute Akashic Records reading combined with 30 minutes of Reiki for $150. Appointments are available in person on Wednesdays in Montclair, NJ, or virtually (and yes, virtual is just as powerful as in-person!).

I’d love to support you on this journey toward clarity and understanding. If you have questions, please don’t hesitate to contact me.
