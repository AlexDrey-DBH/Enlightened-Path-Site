Nurturing Your Spirit as an Empath

journeyMay 20, 2025By Lisa Batitto

Have you ever walked into a room and felt this invisible vibe in the air? Maybe it’s the underlying tension that others might miss, or the sadness that hides behind someone’s bright smile. This is the world of being an empath! Instead of just sensing energy, you’re absorbing it.

It’s pretty normal to find yourself picking up on feelings that aren’t your own. Sometimes you might not notice until your emotions blend with those around you. One minute you’re feeling great, and suddenly, you feel drained or anxious, and it’s not even about your own life!

If this sounds familiar, know you’re not alone; this message is for you.

Let’s Explore Empathy Together

Being an empath is not being overly sensitive. It means your nervous system is finely tuned to the emotions and energies around you, like having your own personal radar! You might feel the pain or joy of others and sense their fears and worries.

It’s important to recognize that this sensitivity is a unique gift that deserves kindness and care.

Step One: Acknowledge Feelings That Aren't Yours

Before trying to “clear” your energy, take a moment to pause, ask yourself: Is this feeling truly mine? If the answer is no or even if you’re unsure, take a deep breath. Picture your body as a vessel. Imagine lifting the lid and gently releasing anything that doesn't belong to you. Give yourself permission to let it go.

You could even say, “If it isn’t mine, I return it.” Feel free to say it out loud; Your body is listening!

Step Two: Protect Yourself with a Soft Shield

You don't want to put up walls to feel safe! Instead, think of creating a gentle filter. Before you enter busy places or jump into heavy conversations, take a minute just for you. Picture yourself grounded, with your feet planted firmly, and see a soft glow wrapping around you, like a cozy screen door that invites in fresh air but keeps out any pesky bugs.

That protective energy is yours to embrace!

Step Three: Make Time for Yourself

As an empath, taking breaks is super important, not because you’re weak, but because your sensitivity is a wonderful part of who you are. Make time for those essential recharge moments! Turn down the noise, wrap yourself in a cozy blanket, enjoy peaceful walks, or rest your hands over your heart, sending yourself love and calm.

Don’t wait until you're completely drained. Make recharging a regular part of your life, just like brushing your teeth!

Empathy is a beautiful gift, and it can thrive when you see it for what it is: a blessing, not a burden. Protect your inner peace, cherish your intuitive nature, and remember that it’s possible to deeply care for others without carrying their weight.

Take care of yourself
