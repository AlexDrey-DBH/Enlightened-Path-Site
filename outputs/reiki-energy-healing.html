What Reiki and energy healing are

A calm practice with roots in Japanese tradition.

Reiki is a hands-on, or hands-near-body, energy practice with roots in Japanese tradition. During a session, you rest comfortably while gentle touch or near-touch is used to support your natural sense of balance and calm.

Energy healing, more broadly, refers to this same kind of work: supporting the flow and balance of your energy, whether you are drawn to it as a spiritual practice or simply as a deeply restorative wellness experience.

You do not need any prior experience or belief system to receive Reiki. Most people describe it simply as deeply relaxing, a quiet, restorative time that gives the mind and body room to settle.

What a session feels like

Quiet, comfortable, and unhurried.

Sessions typically take place with you resting comfortably while we work with the body's energy in a calm setting. Many clients describe a sense of warmth, lightness, or ease during and after a session.

Sessions typically run about an hour, though length can vary depending on the service.

A note on what Reiki is and is not

Complementary support, with honest boundaries.

Reiki is a complementary practice, not a medical treatment. It does not diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any health condition. Some traditional medical institutions offer Reiki as complementary support alongside conventional care, and it works best as part of the full picture of your care. As with any wellness practice, experiences vary.

Reiki FAQ

What you may want to know first.

Does Reiki hurt or feel strange?

No. Most people find it deeply relaxing. Touch, when used, is always light and non-invasive, and you are welcome to ask questions or request adjustments at any point.

How many sessions will I need?

This varies by person and goal. We will talk about what makes sense for you during your first conversation.

Can Reiki work alongside therapy or medical care?

Many clients use Reiki alongside other care. It is meant to complement, not replace, medical or mental-health treatment.
