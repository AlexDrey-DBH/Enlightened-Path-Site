Credible, grounded, and honest about what a wellness program can do.

You do not need to be convinced that employee well-being matters; you need a program that is credible, grounded, and does not overpromise. That is what we build.

Enlightened Path Healing offers workplace mindfulness and well-being programming rooted in both spiritual practice and an honest reading of the evidence. We will not tell you mindfulness will fix your organization's culture. We will help your team build real tools for stress and engagement, delivered by someone who takes both science and spirituality seriously.

What the evidence shows

What programs may include

Start small or build a sustained engagement.

Kickstart

Short term

Ongoing

A comprehensive engagement
