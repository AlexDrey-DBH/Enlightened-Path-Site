Have you ever worked with an energy healer?

If you haven't yet but are curious, the Healer Next Door (aka me!) is here to help you decide if it’s right for you. There are many reasons why you might be thinking of reaching out to an energy healer. For instance:

"Someone I love broke up with me and I don’t know what to do."

"I lost my job because of COVID-19 and I'm having trouble finding a new one."

"I haven't seen my family in more than a year and I am lonely."

These days, most of my clients connect with me because they miss something or someone. These losses are stressful, and stress can cause physical symptoms such as back pain or sleep deprivation. Energy healing can release the stress that causes the discomfort, leaving you feeling better! While it’s not a substitute for a doctor’s care, it can support other medical therapies.

In addition to releasing physical symptoms, energy work can also heal mental, spiritual, and emotional pain. The healing comes when you turn a negative reaction to something that happened to you into an empowering action. As a result, you are confident that you will be ok no matter what happens.

In a future post, I’ll give you tips on how the find the right healer for you.
