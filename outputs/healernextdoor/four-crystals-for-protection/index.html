Four Crystals For Protection

crystalsAugust 9, 2022By Lisa Batitto

There are times in all our lives when we need protection. You may feel stuck in a toxic relationship or anxious from the 24-hour news cycle’s endless stream of gloom and doom. Or you may feel like you are being clouded by negative energy. The truth is, there can be factors, both internal and external, that we may need protection from. If you are dealing with self-negativity, self-sabotage, toxic relationships, or negativity from others, crystals can help.

Crystals can not only be used to protect you from outside sources and negative energies, but they can also help you harness the strength to protect yourself and the clarity to know when to walk away. Below is a list of 4 crystals to help protect you. You can harness these crystals’ energies by holding them in your hand during meditation or wearing them throughout the day. I hope you use the power of crystals to find peace, love, and balance in your life.

  1. Hematite: This crystal is beneficial when used during a transitional or transformational period of life. Its grounding and balancing energies can be helpful when it comes to cultivating a solid foundation during challenging times. Hematite is represented by the element Earth, the zodiac signs of Aquarius and Leo, and the Root Chakra.

  2. Black Onyx: This crystal can be used for manifesting strength and self-confidence, two of the tools you will use to stand up and protect yourself. With the energy of this powerful crystal, you will be able to stand up to whatever life throws at you. It is represented by the element of Earth, the zodiac signs Gemini and Scorpio, and the Root Chakra.

  3. Malachite: This crystal is perfect for repelling negative energy. Whether this energy is external or internal, this crystal can work to ward off all negativity. Malachite is represented by the element of water, the zodiac signs Taurus and Scorpio, and the Heart Chakra.

  4. Amethyst: Amethyst is one of the best crystals for psychic protection. It has stabilizing and balancing properties and can be used to take care of both your emotional and spiritual well-being. Use this crystal to help clear irrational, harmful, or anxious thoughts. It resonates with both the Third Eye and Crown chakras. It can be especially powerful for those born under the signs of Virgo, Capricorn, Aquarius, and Pisces.
