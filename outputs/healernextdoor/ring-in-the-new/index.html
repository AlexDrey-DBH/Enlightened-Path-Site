If you are interested in studying with me, I’m going to be offering Reiki Levels 1 & 2 and Master-Teacher level classes in the new year. The training has been led by Dr. Tony Nadar, the leader of the Transcendental Meditation movement, and I will be among the first graduates of this program. Certification day is January 12 and afterward. I’ll let you know how you can access tools to guide you along your path to enlightenment.
