What the Akashic Records are

A spiritual record of your soul's journey.

Think of the Akashic Records as a spiritual record of your soul's journey: the experiences, patterns, and lessons that have shaped who you are. An Akashic Records reading is a way of accessing that record to bring insight to a question or situation you are currently experiencing.

You do not need to be familiar with this practice beforehand. Many clients come with a specific question; others come simply wanting perspective on a pattern they keep noticing in their life.

Intuition, explained

Inner knowing, reflection, and spiritual insight.

Reading the Records draws on inner knowing, reflection, and spiritual insight, not guesswork. We will talk through what resonates with you.

What a reading is like

Grounded language and room for questions.

During a reading, we open the Records with intention and reflection, then share what comes through in grounded, understandable language, never in a way designed to confuse or overwhelm. You will have space to ask questions throughout.

Ancestral healing

Exploring the patterns and stories a family can carry.

Some readings touch on ancestral patterns, themes that seem to run in a family line. We approach this as reflective spiritual exploration: a way of understanding inherited patterns and stories.

Akashic Records FAQ

Questions before your reading.

Do I need to prepare anything?

You may receive a deeper reading if you have questions or an area of focus in mind.

Is this like a psychic reading?

It is related but distinct. An Akashic Records reading focuses on your soul's history and patterns rather than predicting future events.

Can this be done virtually?

Yes. Akashic Records readings work well virtually, and Lisa offers online sessions for clients wherever they are.
