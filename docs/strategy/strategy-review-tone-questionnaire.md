# Enlightened Path Healing: Strategy Review and Tone Questionnaire

Status: For approval before copy rewriting or structural implementation

## Executive recommendation

Approve a hybrid strategy: use the bolder, distinctive brand position in high-attention messaging, while using a search-led information architecture underneath it.

In practice, that means:

- Keep the current visual identity and refreshed interface as the foundation.
- Let "The spiritual healer for logical people" express the brand difference.
- Let Reiki in Montclair, Akashic Records readings, and meditation supply the clearest search and service signals.
- Retain Work Trauma Coaching and Corporate Wellness as meaningful growth areas without asking them to carry organic acquisition before the data supports that role.
- Preserve Lisa's warm, personal presence while speaking as a founder-led organization where appropriate.

The analytics do not support replacing the current project. They support refining its hierarchy, adding a missing meditation pathway, strengthening exact search relevance, and making the migration technically safe.

## What the evidence says

The supplied analysis uses daily averages because the 2025 data covers a full year and the 2026 data ends July 11. It reports directional increases of 45% in sessions, 23% in page views, and 67% in new users. It also reports a decline in calculated average engagement per landing-page session from about 23.7 seconds to 9.5 seconds. That decline should not be treated as proof of a copy or design failure until tracking, traffic quality, device mix, and page performance are audited.

Organic search produced 21,494 impressions and 284 clicks from January 1 through July 11, 2026, with a 1.32% click-through rate and average position of 15.52. The immediate opportunity is therefore not simply "more traffic." It is better click-through, stronger landing-page relevance, and reliable conversion measurement.

### Evidence-based service hierarchy

1. **Reiki is the protected acquisition engine.** It has the strongest verified local and service-intent demand, including top-10 positions for several Montclair and "near me" searches.
2. **Akashic Records is the clearest emerging opportunity.** Its query set is smaller but already producing clicks and promising positions for a newer service.
3. **Meditation is underrepresented in the current build.** Verified searches show useful rankings and strong click-through for local meditation terms, yet there is no dedicated meditation service page.
4. **Work Trauma Coaching is strategically differentiated but not yet search-proven.** It should remain, with accurate coaching boundaries and a content-building plan.
5. **Corporate Wellness is a longer-horizon B2B opportunity.** It should remain in the architecture, but its weak current engagement and distant search positions argue for a focused page rather than disproportionate homepage emphasis.

## Live site versus current project

### What the current project improves and should keep

- Simpler primary navigation and a more understandable booking path.
- Clearer explanations for skeptical or first-time visitors.
- Stronger boundaries around medical, mental-health, legal, and financial claims.
- More grounded service language than the live site's broad promises.
- Better founder credibility through Lisa's background, credentials, interviews, and award.
- A cleaner, warmer, white-forward visual system that remains recognizably Enlightened Path Healing.
- A low-pressure CTA pattern built around discovery and fit.

### What needs correction before launch

1. **The blog migration required full-content restoration and is now resolved.** All 253 article URLs now contain their original body content and metadata. The 335 legacy category/tag rows have been consolidated as `noindex,follow` pages canonicalized to the Healer Next Door hub. The migration baseline and audit must remain part of launch QA.
2. **The current service hierarchy underuses meditation demand.** Meditation appears inside other pages but lacks its own service landing page.
3. **Core pages are semantically elegant but sometimes too indirect for search.** The Reiki H1 does not name Reiki, the Akashic page lacks local and ancestral language, and the homepage body does not clearly reinforce the Montclair service focus expressed in its title.
4. **Most core pages lack canonical tags and structured data.** Resources and Healer Next Door currently duplicate one another and share the same canonical destination.
5. **The redirect plan changes established clean URLs to `.html` URLs.** Where the deployment platform allows it, established clean paths should remain the public URLs instead.
6. **The sitemap includes placeholder archives and a mixture of clean and `.html` destinations.** It should contain only canonical, indexable pages that are ready to rank.
7. **Conversion tracking is not implementation-ready.** The discovery form has no configured sheet endpoint and falls back to the visitor's email application. No GA4 or tag-manager implementation is present in the current static build.
8. **Blog cards still link to the live-site copies.** The final content system must route them to the repository-owned or CMS-owned canonical articles after migration.

## Two strategic routes

### Route A: Search-led evolution

This is the more conservative option.

- Homepage leads with Reiki and Akashic Records in Montclair.
- Service navigation follows measured demand: Reiki, Akashic Records, Meditation, Work Trauma, Corporate Wellness.
- "The spiritual healer for logical people" appears as a supporting brand statement rather than the main H1.
- Copy gives local terms and service definitions more prominence.

**Advantage:** Safest alignment with verified acquisition behavior.

**Tradeoff:** Less immediate differentiation from other local Reiki and energy-healing providers.

### Route B: Brand-led repositioning

This is the bolder option.

- Homepage hero leads with "The spiritual healer for logical people."
- The journey begins with visitor needs, skepticism, and desired outcomes rather than modality names.
- Lisa's practical, founder-led approach becomes the central differentiator.
- Search language appears immediately below the hero and on tightly focused service pages.

**Advantage:** More ownable, memorable, and emotionally precise.

**Tradeoff:** Requires disciplined supporting copy so Google and first-time visitors still understand the services and location quickly.

### Approved route: Brand-led message, search-led structure

Use the brand-led approach for the homepage's emotional framing, founder story, and voice. Use the search-led approach for navigation, service-page architecture, titles, metadata, internal links, and content priorities. The homepage should lead with an understandable promise rather than make "The spiritual healer for logical people" its primary headline. That line may appear selectively in professional or B2B contexts where it helps explain the grounded approach.

## Proposed page and structure plan

### Home

**Role:** Distinctive brand introduction and route to the right service.

**Recommended flow:** Brand hero; grounded proof line naming Reiki, Akashic Records, meditation, Montclair, and virtual availability; audience pathways; service hierarchy; Lisa and credibility; how it works; testimonials; selected resources; discovery CTA.

**Copy direction:** Keep "The spiritual healer for logical people" as the leading option pending the tone questionnaire. Do not force keywords into the headline. Place exact service and location language in the supporting paragraph and first content section.

### Services

**Role:** Help visitors choose by need while giving every core service a clear internal-link destination.

**Recommended order:** Reiki; Akashic Records; Meditation; Work Trauma Coaching; Corporate Wellness.

**Structure:** Need-based selector; five service summaries; comparison guidance; scope boundaries; discovery CTA.

### Reiki and Energy Healing

**Role:** Highest-priority local acquisition page.

**Search focus:** Reiki Montclair NJ, Reiki healing, Reiki session, Reiki practitioner, Reiki Master Teacher, energy healing, virtual Reiki.

**Structure:** Search-clear hero; plain-language definition; what a session is like; who it may support; Lisa's qualifications; in-person and virtual details; carefully framed institutional context; FAQ; booking CTA.

### Akashic Records

**Role:** Emerging acquisition and education page.

**Search focus:** Akashic Records reading, Akashic reading, local and virtual reading terms, ancestral healing as a secondary concept.

**Structure:** Clear definition; questions people bring; session process; skepticism and boundaries; ancestral-pattern explanation; Lisa's qualifications; FAQ; CTA.

### Meditation

**Role:** Capture verified local demand that the current architecture does not serve directly.

**Search focus:** Meditation Montclair, meditation classes near me, guided meditation, mindfulness classes, virtual meditation.

**Structure:** Practical, beginner-friendly hero; class and individual options; what to expect; relationship to Reiki; local and virtual availability; events or workshops; CTA.

### Work Trauma Coaching

**Role:** Own the clearest differentiated problem space while building search visibility over time.

**Search focus:** Work trauma coaching, workplace stress, burnout recovery, toxic workplace recovery, healing after workplace stress.

**Structure:** Recognition of the experience; coaching scope; practical outcomes; program options only if confirmed current; Lisa's relevant experience; related resources; CTA.

### Corporate Wellness

**Role:** Convert HR, leadership, and wellness-program inquiries.

**Search focus:** Corporate mindfulness programs, corporate meditation, employee wellness, team meditation, workplace stress management.

**Structure:** Organizational needs; program formats; logistics; outcomes framed without overpromising; employee feedback; inquiry CTA. Keep the tone more operational than the consumer pages while preserving warmth.

### About Lisa

**Role:** Resolve trust, credibility, and fit.

**Structure:** Founder story; practical philosophy; professional history; qualifications; interviews and award; approach to skepticism; short personal detail; CTA. Keep the current credibility work and refine it after Lisa confirms which credentials remain active.

### Healer Next Door / Resources

**Role:** Editorial authority, internal linking, email growth, and long-tail search acquisition.

**Structure:** One canonical hub at `/healernextdoor`; topic filters based on real content clusters; full article archive in a CMS; useful article-to-service pathways; author and updated dates; newsletter CTA.

**First optimization set:** A Guide to Seeing Auras; Meditation and Reiki; Reiki for Kids: What to Expect; The Ethics of Energy Healing; The Hidden Dangers of Toxic Positivity.

### Contact / Begin Here

**Role:** Convert uncertainty into a measurable next step.

**Structure:** Discovery form; Acuity scheduler; location and hours; corporate inquiry route; privacy and consent language; clear confirmation state. Track form start, successful submission, booking-platform click, email click, and confirmed newsletter subscription separately.

## Phased implementation plan

### Phase 0: Measurement and preservation

- Audit GA4 key events and determine what each custom event represents.
- Configure successful inquiry submission, booking clicks, email clicks, and newsletter confirmation as distinct events.
- Export and map every indexable live URL.
- Decide canonical public URLs before rewriting internal links.
- Preserve the completed 253-post migration, local article assets, taxonomy consolidation, and clean Healer Next Door hub through deployment.
- Establish redirect, canonical, sitemap, and indexing test plans.

### Phase 1: Core messaging and conversion

- Resolve the tone questionnaire.
- Approve the brand-led versus search-led balance.
- Rewrite Home, Services, Reiki, Akashic Records, Meditation, Contact, and About.
- Add page titles, descriptions, canonical tags, social metadata, and appropriate schema.
- Connect and test the discovery form and Acuity journey.

### Phase 2: Growth services and editorial content

- Rewrite Work Trauma and Corporate Wellness.
- Restore and optimize the five priority articles.
- Build a single regional service-area section.
- Add contextual internal links from relevant legacy articles to service pages.
- Consolidate duplicate and misspelled tags without changing valuable article URLs.

### Phase 3: Post-launch optimization

- Establish a clean 30-day measurement baseline.
- Compare click-through rate, qualified inquiries, booking starts, and completed bookings by landing page.
- Test titles and descriptions on high-impression, low-CTR pages.
- Reconsider city pages or a dedicated virtual-services page only when demand and content depth justify them.
- Expand corporate and work-trauma content after real query and lead signals appear.
