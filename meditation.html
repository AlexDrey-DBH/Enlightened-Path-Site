The idea

More space between a feeling and a reaction.

Meditation is a practical tool for noticing what is happening in your mind and body, and building a little more space between a feeling and a reaction. Mindfulness is that same awareness carried into daily life: at your desk, in a hard conversation, or in the middle of a stressful day.

We teach these practices the way we would want them taught to us: clearly, without spiritual bypassing, and adapted to your actual life, not an idealized version of it.

Meditation FAQ

Questions from people getting started.

I have tried meditation before and could not stick with it. Will this be different?

It can be, because we build practices around your actual life and attention span, not a one-size-fits-all routine.

Is mindfulness a religious practice?

Mindfulness, as taught here, is a practical, secular tool that draws on contemplative traditions. You do not need any particular spiritual belief to benefit.
