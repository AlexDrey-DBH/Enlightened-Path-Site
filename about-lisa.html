My journey

Learning to pay attention to what I once brushed aside.

From a young age, people often told me I had "healing hands." At first, I brushed it off, figuring my hands were like anyone else's. But as I grew up, I kept noticing that people would comment on my calming energy. One moment that stayed with me was when a metaphysician told me I had an unusually large aura. It was a small thing that made me smile, but it became part of what led me to pay real attention to these gifts.

Before founding Enlightened Path Healing, I spent many years working in public relations and social media for nonprofit and arts organizations. During the COVID-19 lockdown, I took time to reflect and began pursuing certifications in Reiki, meditation, and subconscious mind coaching. In June 2020, I founded Enlightened Path Healing as a space where people could receive grounded, compassionate support on their healing journeys.

My philosophy

Healing is not fixing someone.

It is helping people reconnect with their own inner strength, clarity, and capacity to move forward. My role is to offer support, insight, and practical tools while honoring that each person's path is their own.

I bring the same discipline to spiritual work that I would bring to anything else worth doing well: training, boundaries, and honesty about what this work can and cannot do. I want you to leave a session with something useful, whether that is greater understanding, a deeper sense of calm, or a next step that feels more manageable.

How we can work together

Distinct services, shaped around what you need.

My own path taught me how deeply difficult workplace experiences, relationships, and long-standing patterns can shape the way we see ourselves and the choices we make. That realization led me to look more closely at my past, understand the patterns I was repeating, and explore practices that could help me relate to those experiences differently.

Over time, I studied with Reiki masters, intuitives, and other healing practitioners, bringing together spiritual practice, grounded reflection, and tools people can carry into daily life.

Today, I offer Reiki, Akashic Records readings, meditation, and Work Trauma Coaching as distinct services. Depending on what you are navigating, we can focus on relaxation and energetic balance, explore recurring patterns and questions, develop a personalized meditation practice, or work through the aftereffects of a difficult professional experience.
